The "Viral Cast Photo" Formula: 10 Prompts for Group Shots

From the "Vanity Fair" cover to the candid "Oscar Selfie," here is how to generate perfect ensemble cast images.

Generating a consistent group of characters is one of the hardest tasks in AI art. The AI often struggles to make everyone look related or part of the same "universe." The secret? Framing the prompt as a specific type of photograph.

Whether you want a gritty 90s grunge band look or a polished Hollywood ensemble, use the generator below to customize your cast.

âš¡ How to Handle "Real" Actors

AI struggles to generate 10 specific celebrities in one shot (e.g., "Brad Pitt, Tom Cruise, Leo DiCaprio...") because it blends their faces.

  • Method 1 (The Archetype): Instead of names, describe the types. E.g., "A diverse cast of 5 attractive actors in tuxedos, one man with long hair, one woman with a bob cut..." This creates a cohesive, realistic group.
  • Method 2 (The Lookalike): Use names of obscure actors or mix distinctive features. E.g., "A man resembling a young Harrison Ford next to a woman with Zendaya's style..."
  • Method 3 (The Pro Swap): Generate the group shot using Method 1 to get the composition right. Then, use a Face Swap tool (like InsightFace) to replace the AI faces with the specific real actors you want.
