AI struggles to generate 10 specific celebrities in one shot because it blends their faces.

  • Method 1 (The Archetype): Instead of names, describe the types (e.g., "A cast of 5 attractive actors in tuxedos"). This creates a cohesive group.
  • Method 2 (The Lookalike): Use comparisons (e.g., "A man resembling a young Harrison Ford").
  • Method 3 (The Pro Swap): Generate the group shot using Method 1 to get the composition right. Then, use a Face Swap tool (like InsightFace) to replace the AI faces with the specific real actors you want.
